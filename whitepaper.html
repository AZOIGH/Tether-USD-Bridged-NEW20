Litepaper

Overview

TEHTER USDT ERC20 is a community effort to provide USD‑pegged liquidity across multiple networks via bridging infrastructure. This litepaper outlines goals, risks, and high‑level design. It is not investment advice.

1. Motivation

Stable settlement across chains is fragmented. TEHTER USDT ERC20 aims to simplify access while clearly disclosing bridge and market risks.

2. Design

  • Bridged representation with transparent supply accounting.
  • Multi‑sig operations and public reporting cadence.
  • Ecosystem‑first incentives and open interfaces.

3. Risk Disclosures

  • Smart contract/bridge risk (bugs, downtime, custody issues).
  • Market/peg risk due to liquidity conditions.
  • Regulatory/compliance obligations vary by jurisdiction.

4. Roadmap

Initial integrations, audit engagement, and liquidity programs, followed by expanded chain support and reporting dashboards.
